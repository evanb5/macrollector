FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need a Macrollector account to use the app?

A: No, you do not need an account to use Macrollector. However, getting a Macrollector account will grant you access to additional features from the app such as daily macronutrient counting and dietary planning advice. These features are helpful to those who are struggling with sticking to a healthy diet, and are completely free.

Q: Can I connect my Macrollector app to other health apps or my email?

A: For now, Macrollector is not connectable to other health apps and personal emails. However, we are looking into this possibility to make Macrollector more efficient and accessible to our users in the near future.

Q: Can Macrollector recognize vegan variations of food items?

A: No, Macrollector is not designed to take vegan food items into consideration during identification. This includes vegan variations of common foods such as vegan pork, vegan beef, vegan fish, and others.

Q: Can Macrollector's advice take health conditions into account?

A: No, Macrollector is not designed to take into consideration conditions that may change or affect one's daily nutritional intake when providing nutritional advice. Please follow any dietary instructions given by a medical expert if you are altering your diet due to a health condition.

Q: My Macrollector app isn't recognizing the food I take a picture of.

A: If there is an issue with Macrollector's recognition system it may be due to a variety of different factors. Make sure the image you have taken is clear and the food is unobstructed by anything in the photo. The issue may also be occuring due to the type of food you are taking a picture of. If the food is relatively uncommon or not for sale in normal stores, then it may not be in our recognizable database of food items. If you have any other concerns about the system, ask us below and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
